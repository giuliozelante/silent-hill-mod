The MilkShape 3D Community

The MilkShape 3D community is a continually growing archive of knowledge and resources. Users are encouraged to ask questions and answer them, should they have the experience. The web site for MilkShape 3D can be accessed at http://www.swissquake.ch/chumbalum-soft.

The forums can be accessed at the main Chumbalum-Soft internet site, from which you can also find a great deal of MilkShape 3D related tutorials and resources, as well as the latest downloads and news on new versions of MilkShape 3D.

I strongly recommend visiting the forums on a regular basis if you like MilkShape 3D. A great deal of MilkShape 3D information and fun stuff is available. You can also browse other user's work, upload your own work, have a pole based on questions you set and judge other user's work.
