Build Time: Since ive began mapping. (about one year)

Installation Info: First you'll have to unzip the files to somewhere at the HD
(e.g. C:\Developing\Prefabs)
There are two ways to use these prefabs on your map, first either click
"Insert document..." under the "File" menu; or (and the best way) open the
Prefab clicking "open" under the "File" menu, select the mesh/mesh's you want
to use (in Transform mode (f5)) and press Ctrl+C, then close the window, now,
in your map, in Model mode (F3) put the cursor where you want to put the prefab
and press Ctrl+V.

Bugs: None that i'm aware of. Contact me plz if you find any.

Obs: Some of the prefabs names are in portuguese (my language), sorry about that.
