GeoSphere Primitive

A GeoSphere is made from a more natural structure than a normal sphere. This structure is found in nature as it offers optimum strength (more than the structure of a sphere) at a lower cost.

A GeoSphere is created with one parameter, which is depth. You should experiment with this value to achieve a complexity you are after. You will notice that you can achieve the same visual smoothness in a GeoSphere that you can in a sphere with fewer polygons.
