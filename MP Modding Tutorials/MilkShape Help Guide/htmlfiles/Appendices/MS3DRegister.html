Registering MilkShape 3D

MilkShape 3D is a trial based program that requres registration for it to become fully functional outside of its 30 day trial limit period.

Registration of the 1.x version series of MilkShape 3D costs 20 US Dollars (25 Euros). With a credit card you can register MilkShape 3D online at the Chumbalum-Soft web site at the address:

http://www.swissquake.ch/chumbalum-soft/ms3d/register.html

MilkShape 3D can also be ordered through the post by printing and filling out a simple order form.
