The Toolbox

The Toolbox is the center of MilkShape; it is from this component that the majority of functions relating to the construction of objects is carried out.

There are four tabs in the Toolbox all relating to the various aspects of model creation, these are:

  • Model

This tab is used for the placement of vertices and the construction of polygons and skeletons.
  • Groups

This tab contains features related to the grouping of vertices and polygons. Groups can also be created from existing polygons.
  • Materials

Deals with the creation of materials (including textures from file) ready to be assigned to groups.
  • Joints

Contains tools relating to the manipulation of joints in skeletons.
