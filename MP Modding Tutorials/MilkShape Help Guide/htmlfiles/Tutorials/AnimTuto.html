Tutorial: Animation

 

There are three parts to this tutorial.

  • Part 1: Setting up the Skeleton
  • Part 2: Assigning Vertices to Bones
  • Part 3: Creating a Walk Animation

Introduction

This tutorial deals with the walking animation of a biped model, but the principals can be applied to any form of animation. A good animation can be made purely by trial and error, the process takes a while but the results will look better, if you tweak certain frames. The correct positioning of joints also adds a lot to the final product so spend some time acquainting yourself with human physiology.

The length of time this tutorial takes to complete will depend on your experience and understanding of the concepts involved.
