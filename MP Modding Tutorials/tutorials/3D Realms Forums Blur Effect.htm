Kozak

 
Re: Blur Effect
    06/06/03 03:51 AM

Projectiles are no particle effects. Particle efefcts are 3d projections of 3d bitmaps. A projectile is real 3d (i.e. the ghosting effect of a dodging agent in TRW is a 3d projectile agent with a semitransparent texture).

Projectiles are KF2 files thus geometry stuff which can be made with Milkshape or 3dsmax.
