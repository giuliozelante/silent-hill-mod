Deflex
Re: How to animate Weapons in 3dsMax?
Ok i was in a real hurry last time. sry for that.

Ok this is an explanation how to make a most common gun animation. Its the slide animation im talking about.

First go to the fifth button from the right in the lower right corner. Holding the mouse over it should say "<font color="red">Time Configuration</font>". Click it, select End Time to 5 and click ok.

Now youre animation has 5 frames. Next to the key button is an "<font color="red">Auto Key</font>" click that so it highlights. Now move the Time Slider wich is down on the red stripe that should appere when you pressed Auto Key. Move it to 2.

Now move the gun slide where you want it (now that this is a gun it should be to the right or left if you have the weapon in the left view) .

When this is done you will se that you have 2 new frames. One on frame 0 and one on frame 2. Drag the mouse over the one on frame 0 and press and hold shift, drag it to the last fifth frame. Thats all.

Export only with Animation checked in the middle section. I think you can have the other like they allready are. Hope this helps you and that you understood my english. If you still having problems contact me on deflex1@hotmail.com
